About MEDITECH

A national celebration of ideas, innovation, and student entrepreneurship — workshops, talks, and networking. MEDITECH 2K26 is the flagship National Level Technical Symposium organized by India's first Biomedical Engineering Department (est. 1982) at Osmania University. This two-day event converges 300+ participants including students, industry professionals, clinicians, and researchers to advance healthcare technology innovation. With a rich legacy of fostering biomedical engineering talent, MEDITECH 2K26 offers a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange, skill development, and networking. Attendees can engage in hands-on workshops, insightful talks, and a prestigious BoardRoom competition, all designed to inspire and empower the next generation of healthcare innovators. Join us in shaping the future of medical technology at MEDITECH 2K26!.
